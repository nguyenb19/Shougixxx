Actions for Shogi game


-select (and show coordinates), deselect, move, place, promote a Shogi piece

-capture other player's Shogi piece

-undo a move (certain amount per game)

-select, deselect, place a new (captured) Shogi piece on the board


-restart game
-surrender and/or quit game

-select options
	
-check records
	
-toggle music
	
-choose mode
